# HelloPrint Brand Identity

This is the narrative complement to `tokens.json`. When a value in prose here ever disagrees with `tokens.json`, **tokens.json wins** — fix the prose.

## Who we are

HelloPrint is Europe's B2B marketplace for customisable printed products — business cards, flyers, banners, merch, packaging, clothing. Founded in 2013 in Rotterdam.

- **Mission** — Make print easy for every business, so anyone can print anything, anywhere.
- **Vision** — Be the default print operating system of Europe.
- **Position** — The easiest way to print, backed by a vetted supplier network and real human support.
- **Audience** — SMB owners, marketers, designers, and procurement leads who need print to be predictable, fast, and fair-priced.

## Personality

HelloPrint is the friend who happens to know everything about print. Knowledgeable without being stuffy. Direct without being cold. Confident without performing.

Picture a man in his early thirties who started his first business at 26 — grounded, warm, assured, measured. He says what he means, shows his work, and doesn't waste your time.

## Six values

1. **Think Like A Customer** — Every decision starts from the customer's point of view. If it's not obviously good for them, we don't ship it.
2. **Own It** — No hand-offs, no "that's not my team." If you see it, you own it until it's resolved.
3. **Do It Together** — Better decisions come from surfacing disagreement early. Bring the hard question to the room.
4. **Raise the Bar** — Yesterday's good is today's table stakes. Ship, learn, improve, repeat.
5. **Celebrate Success** — Name the wins. Name the people. Small and public beats big and quiet.
6. **AI-First Thinking** — Default to "Can Claude do this?" before hiring, outsourcing, or building. Humans design the system; AI runs it.

## Visual identity at a glance

- **Primary colour** — Charmed Green `#008539`. Used as an accent, not as body fill (except on OOH).
- **Dark panel** — Thamar Black `#022B07` — a deep green-black. This is our "lights off" surface.
- **Light canvas** — Snowflake `#F0F0F0` or pure `#FFFFFF`. The default for product and doc UI.
- **Digital black is `#191919`, not `#000000`.** Pure black looks harsh on screens and isn't us.
- **Typography** — Inter, everywhere. Weights 400 / 600 / 700. See `tokens.json` for the full scale.
- **Logo** — Wordmark is the default mark. Monograms (circular H) are for small spaces only — app icons, favicons, avatars.
- **Handwritten (Smiley)** — Reserved for greetings and sign-offs. Never for prices, guarantees, legal, or body copy.

## The 60 / 30 / 10 hierarchy

Every layout — slide, page, email, screen — should respect this:

- **60%** — Dominant surface (white, snowflake, or thamar black).
- **30%** — Supporting structure (text, dividers, secondary panels).
- **10%** — Accent. Charmed Green drives most of this (≥7%); a secondary accent can take the remaining ≤3% if it adds clarity, never decoration.

If green is doing 40% of the work, the layout is wrong. Pull it back.

## Background variants

Four canvases are approved. Don't invent new ones.

1. **Full dark green** (`#022B07`) — bold statement, product hero, dark-mode UI.
2. **Split white + dark green strip** — common for page headers, footers, slide dividers.
3. **Full light / white** — default for documents, long-read UI, Excel, most decks.
4. **Full Charmed Green** (`#008539`) — OOH (billboards, large print, stands) **only**. Not for screens with reading-length content.

## What not to do

- Don't use pure `#000000` in digital. Use Digital Black `#191919`.
- Don't stretch, recolour, rotate, or stroke the logo.
- Don't let green run wild. Charmed Green is an accent.
- Don't use Smiley for anything other than greetings / sign-offs.
- Don't write the brand name as "Helloprint". It is always **HelloPrint** (one word, camel-cased H and P).
