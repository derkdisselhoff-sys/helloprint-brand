## Personality

Three words: **Friendly · Pragmatic · Expert**

- **Friendly** — warm, human, never corporate or cold
- **Pragmatic** — direct, useful, no fluff
- **Expert** — confident and knowledgeable, without being intimidating

---

## Voice & Tone

**Default register:** Friendly · Expert · Human

| Context | How we sound |
|---|---|
| Marketing & ecommerce | Playful, confident, energetic — benefit-led |
| Transactional / product | Clear, calm, structured — nothing ambiguous |
| Customer service | Warm, direct, reassuring |
| OOH / billboard | Bold, short, ALL CAPS — 3–4 words maximum |

**Writing principles:**

- Lead with the main message — no warm-up preamble
- One idea per section
- Short paragraphs, clear hierarchy, bullets for scannability
- Clarity beats cleverness — always

**Never:**

- Jargon or unexplained print terminology
- Salesy, aggressive, or overhyped language
- Vague promises or weasel words
- Lengthy sign-offs or corporate filler

For the full voice treatment, see [`02-voice.md`](./02-voice.md).

## Visual Identity

### Colour

| Role | Name | Token | Hex |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary green | Charmed Green | `color.brand.charmed-green` | `#008539` |
| Primary dark | Thamar Black | `color.brand.thamar-black` | `#022B07` |
| Primary link green | Link Green | `color.brand.link-green` | `#049E46` |
| Light / base | Snowflake | `color.brand.snowflake` | `#F0F0F0` |
| Neutral | Orochimaru | `color.brand.orochimaru` | `#D9D9D9` |
| **Accent** — use max 1 per asset | Heroic Red | `color.accent.heroic-red` | `#D64545` |
| **Accent** | Honey Teriyaki | `color.accent.honey-teriyaki` | `#F36D13` |
| **Accent** | Ripe Mango | `color.accent.ripe-mango` | `#FDC225` |
| **Accent** | Hampton Beach | `color.accent.hampton-beach` | `#9D6639` |
| **Accent** | Seaside | `color.accent.seaside` | `#67A5B1` |

**Rules:** Never combine accent colours. Never use more than one accent per asset. Charmed Green (`color.brand.charmed-green`) must appear on every ad asset.

### Background system

Four variants — pick one, never blend. Token path: `color.background.*`.

| Variant | Token | Treatment | Use for |
|---|---|---|---|
| Full dark green | `full-dark-green` | `#022B07` | B2B, high-impact, promotional |
| Split white + dark green | `split-white-strip` | White top ~60% / `#022B07` bottom | Product / seasonal |
| Full light / white | `full-light` | White or `#F0F0F0` | Lifestyle, soft tone, logo-led |
| Full HelloPrint Green | `full-charmed-green` | `#008539` | Billboards, OOH only |

### Typography

| Role | Font | Weight | Token |
|---|---|---|---|
| Headlines | Inter | Bold (700) | `font.weight.bold` |
| Sub-headlines | Inter | Semi Bold (600) | `font.weight.semi-bold` |
| Body / UI | Inter | Regular (400) | `font.weight.regular` |
| Greetings / sign-offs | Smiley (handwritten) | — | `font.family.handwritten` |

Never use Smiley for prices, guarantees, or legal content.

### Logo

- **Wordmark:** HelloPrint script — primary brand mark
- **Monogram:** H (circular) — secondary, used at small sizes
- White version on dark/green backgrounds · Dark version on light backgrounds
- Minimum clear space: equal to the height of the "H" on all sides

See [`05-logos.md`](./05-logos.md) for files and placement rules.

### Layout (ad assets)

Five zones, always present:

| Zone | Element |
|---|---|
| Top left (landscape) / top centre (square) | Logo — white or dark wordmark |
| Top right | Offer badge — circular, `#008539` fill |
| Middle left | Headline — Inter Bold, large, left-aligned |
| Bottom right | Product mockup — fictional SMB logo, never HelloPrint-branded |
| Bottom left | CTA — green pill button |

Trust signals (B Corp · Trustpilot · 100% satisfaction) grouped at the very bottom on B2B formats.
